Thank you to all the contestants!

What an incredible contest! We had over 80 videos submitted and just about 11,000 votes cast. Thank you so much to all of our contestants for taking the time to make and produce a video, and for having the guts to enter a nationwide contest and share with your friends! We were so happy with the quality of the submissions, and so were our judges! Money in politics is a tough issue to tackle, especially in 30 seconds, and these videos were dynamic in both their quality and message.

A special thanks to our partners MoveOn.org, PCCC and Rootstrikers!
