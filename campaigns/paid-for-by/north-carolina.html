Get out the vote in North Carolina!

MAYDAY is backing anti-corruption candidate Walter Jones in North Carolina’s 3rd Congressional District (NC-03). Representative Jones is the only Republican Member of Congress to publicly support reform. He’s a co-sponsor of the Government by the People Act, and the only Republican to support the DISCLOSE Act.
